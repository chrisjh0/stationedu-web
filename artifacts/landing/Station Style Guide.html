Station · Visual Language v1

A campus that finally feels like one place.

Station moves away from the single corporate blue toward a grounded Ink Navy anchor and a warm Coral signature — supported by a muted, color-coded palette so every club, sport, and society has a place to live. Clean and modern, but unmistakably Station.

Ink Navy
--primary · #232E54
Primary brand & UI anchor. Buttons, nav, headings, structure. Reads as a near-black with depth — not "another SaaS blue."
Coral
--accent · Coral
The warm signature. Primary CTAs, active states, highlights, focus rings.
interests

Warm meets cool

Cool slate neutrals carry the UI; warm Coral carries the energy. The tension is the personality.

palette

Color with a job

Seven muted category hues turn a wall of clubs into something scannable — color as wayfinding, not decoration.

dark_mode

Built for both

Every token has a real dark-mode value. Try the toggle, top-right — nothing is faked.

01 — Color

A palette that does more than fill space

Two brand colors, a cool neutral spine, seven category hues, and four status colors. That's the whole system — restrained on purpose so the category colors stay meaningful.

03 — Shape & Elevation
