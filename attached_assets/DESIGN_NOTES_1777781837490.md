## Brand & Style

This design system is built for the modern educational landscape, balancing the administrative rigor of a B2B platform with the high-energy environment of student life. The brand personality is **authoritative yet frictionless**, positioning itself as a silent facilitator that clears the way for student engagement.

The visual style is a hybrid of **Minimalism** and **Glassmorphism**. By removing borders and relying on negative space and depth, the interface achieves a "canvas" feel where content is the primary focus. The use of semi-transparent layers suggests a modern, tech-forward sophistication, while the "Professional Education Blue" palette ensures the platform remains grounded and trustworthy for school administrators.

## Colors

The palette is anchored by **Deep Navy (#0F172A)** for high-contrast text and structural elements, and **Electric Blue (#2563EB)** for action and identity. 

- **Primary Action:** Use the Electric Blue, often applied as a subtle linear gradient to give buttons a sense of "lift."
- **Surfaces:** The base canvas uses **Soft Slate (#F8FAFC)**. Glass surfaces should use a white base with 70% opacity and a high blur radius.
- **Feedback:** Use standard semantic colors (Success Emerald, Error Rose) but muted to fit the professional tone.

## Typography

The system utilizes a dual-font strategy. **Lexend** is reserved for headings and display text to evoke a clear, modern, and accessible educational feel. **Inter** is used for all body copy, inputs, and system labels to ensure maximum legibility in data-heavy management views.

Hierarchy is established through weight and scale rather than color shifts. Large headlines should use a slightly tighter letter spacing to maintain a cohesive visual block against the wide whitespace.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a **Fixed-Fluid Hybrid** model. The sidebar remains at a fixed width (280px) while the main "Canvas" expands to fill the viewport. Content within the canvas is organized on a 12-column grid with generous 24px gutters.

Whitespace is treated as a first-class structural element. Avoid "crowding" components; use the **Large (40px)** spacing unit to separate major sections of a page. Inner card padding should never drop below **Medium (24px)** to maintain the airy, minimalist aesthetic.

## Elevation & Depth

This design system avoids borders entirely, using **Soft, Diffused Shadows** and **Backdrop Blurs** to define hierarchy.

1.  **Level 0 (Canvas):** The base Soft Slate background.
2.  **Level 1 (Cards/Content):** White surfaces with a very soft shadow (`0px 10px 30px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.04)`).
3.  **Level 2 (Glass Layers):** Sidebar and Floating Modals. These use `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` and a thin, 1px semi-transparent white highlight on the top edge to simulate glass thickness.
4.  **Level 3 (Popovers/Tooltips):** Slightly more aggressive shadows to ensure separation from the glass layers.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by **expansive roundness**. Standard cards and containers use a **24px radius**, while smaller interactive elements like input fields and buttons use a **16px radius**. 

Status indicators and avatar frames may use "Pill" shapes (9999px) to provide a soft contrast to the larger rectangular containers. The goal is to remove all "sharpness" from the interface, making the management experience feel inviting and non-combative.

## Components

- **Sidebar:** A full-height glass panel. Active states are indicated by a "Soft Blue Glow"—a subtle outer shadow in Electric Blue behind the active menu item, combined with a 4px vertical pill indicator on the leading edge.
- **Cards:** Borderless containers. Use high-contrast Lexend for titles and ensure plenty of internal padding (24px+). For nested content, use a slightly darker slate background rather than a stroke.
- **Buttons:** 
    - *Primary:* Gradient fill (Electric Blue to Bright Blue), white text, soft drop shadow matching the button color.
    - *Secondary/Ghost:* No background or border; use Electric Blue text with a subtle background hover state (5% opacity blue).
- **Input Fields:** Soft Slate background (slightly darker than the canvas) with a 16px radius. On focus, the field should glow with a soft blue shadow rather than a thick border.
- **Chips/Badges:** Small, pill-shaped elements using low-opacity versions of the primary color (e.g., 10% blue fill with 100% blue text) to keep the "light" feel.
- **Action Dashboard:** A specialized component for school leads featuring large-scale stats with subtle background gradients to draw the eye to key metrics.
